Our Ramleela Committee is one of the oldest Ramleela committee in Prayagraj district of Uttar Pradesh, which started in the year 1935. After few years of its inception, Ramleela was not staged for a few years due to some social reasons, but again from 1966 AD, it has been continuously staging the grand Ramleela. Thus, this time Ramleela of 2020 is going to be the 55th Ramleela of this committee. Every year Shree Ramleela is organized for 6 days starting from Quar Shukla Saptami to Quar Shukla Dwadashi and a fair is organized on the 7th day.



-: Recent News :-


The committee has successfully played 55th Shree Ramleela with full safety and precautions against Covid-19. So far it is considered as the best staged Ramleela in its history.
